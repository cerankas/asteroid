# Asteroid
A simple game about an asteroid, where the only thing you control is its color.
* Swipe left/right to change your color.
* Asteroids of similar colors attract you.
* Asteroids of opposite colors repel you.
* On contact, smaller asteroids are absorbed by larger ones.
* Your score is 100 x log10 of your current size.
